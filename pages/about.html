About BBN Stats

BBN Stats is the premier statistics website dedicated to University of Kentucky mens basketball. We provide deep insights into the Kentucky Wildcats Basketball Team through comprehensive statistical analysis and advanced metrics.

This platform synthesizes data from official NCAA sources, advanced analytics providers, and proprietary tracking systems to deliver:

  • Player: Player Lists view with sorting and Player Profiles with advanced stats
  • Staff: Full Staff with profiles when clicked
  • Schedule: Kentucky Mens Basketball schedule with box scores when clicked
  • Rankings: Team Rankings including basic and advanced rankings
  • Statistics: Team Statistics including advanced team metrics
  • Compare: Player Comparison including averages and advanced metrics
  • History: Historical Data for the Kentucky Wildcats historic success
  • News: Top News sources for Kentucky Basketball with direct access when clicked on

While independent from the University, BBN Stats upholds the same standard of excellence that defines Kentucky basketball. Website gets updated after every game.
